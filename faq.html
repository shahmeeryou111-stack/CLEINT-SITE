Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our services and processes.

We offer a comprehensive range of construction services including residential construction (custom homes, renovations, additions), commercial construction (office buildings, retail spaces, industrial facilities), land development (site preparation, grading, infrastructure), design services, and project management.

Getting a quote is easy! Simply contact us through our website form, call us at (313) 444-9734, or email us at bula@BULADEV.com. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your project needs, provide an assessment, and give you a detailed, transparent quote.

Yes, BULADEV + ASA Construction is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We maintain all necessary licenses and insurance coverage to protect our clients and ensure compliance with all local and state regulations.

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity of the work. A simple renovation might take 4-8 weeks, while custom home construction can take 6-12 months or more. During our consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed timeline specific to your project.

Absolutely! We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. We offer a 1-year workmanship warranty on all our projects, and many materials come with manufacturer warranties as well. We'll explain all warranty details during your consultation.

We are based in Detroit, Michigan, and serve the greater Detroit metropolitan area and surrounding regions. Contact us to discuss your specific location – we're happy to discuss travel for larger projects.

Yes, we offer comprehensive design services including architectural design, 3D visualization, blueprint creation, and permit acquisition. Our design team works closely with you to bring your vision to life before construction begins.

We understand that changes may arise during construction. We handle change requests professionally, providing clear documentation of any changes, their impact on timeline and budget, and obtaining your approval before proceeding. Communication is key throughout the process.

We offer flexible payment options tailored to each project. Typically, we work with a structured payment schedule tied to project milestones. We'll discuss all payment terms and options during your consultation to find what works best for your situation.
